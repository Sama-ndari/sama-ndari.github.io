Velora: Productivity & Wellness

A personal productivity and wellness app built with Flutter, monetized through a custom device-tied license activation system. Users purchase a subscription (Standard or AI tier) and receive an activation code bound to their device. The entire licensing and admin infrastructure runs serverless via GitHub Gist.

Core Modules:

  • Habit Tracking - Daily, weekly, times-per-week, and monthly habits with streak calculation, calendar heatmap, and detailed statistics
  • Goal Management - Create goals with milestones, track progress, and visualize completion over time
  • Smart Alarms - Background alarm service with snooze, custom sounds, and reliable wake-up even when app is closed
  • Expense Tracking - Log and categorize expenses, track spending patterns, and monitor budgets over time

Key Features:

  • Offline-First - Hive local database, works without internet. Google Drive backup for data safety
  • License Activation - SHA-256 device-bound codes with anti-cheat detection for clock manipulation
  • Remote Admin Panel - Manage clients, generate keys, blacklist devices, and push targeted messages via GitHub Gist
  • Notification System - Smart reminders for habits, goals, alarms, and subscription status
  • Trash & Recovery - Recycle bin for deleted habits, goals, and journal entries with restore capability

Subscription Tiers:

  • Standard - Full access to habits, goals, alarms, journal, and backup
  • AI - Everything in Standard plus AI-powered features and analytics
